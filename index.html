ESR Experiments on a Single Donor Electron in Isotopically Enriched Silicon

April 11, 2016 at 4:00 pm 241 Compton

One of the leading candidates for a solid-state quantum bit is the spin of a single donor electron in silicon. Electron spin coherence times for donors in silicon can be as long as 10 s in isotopically enriched 28Si, an isotope which has zero nuclear spin (A. M. Tyryshkin et al., Nat. Mat. 11, 143 (2012)).
